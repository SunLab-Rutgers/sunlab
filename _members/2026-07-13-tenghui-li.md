Tenghui is a postdoctoral researcher in the Sun Lab. He received his B.S. in 2021 and Ph.D. in 2026 from Zhejiang University under the supervision of Prof. Linjun Wang. During his doctoral studies, he conducted research in computational spectroscopy, machine-learning electronic Hamiltonians, and nonadiabatic dynamics simulations in extended systems, with a particular focus on quantum dots. His current research focuses on developing computational approaches for modeling and simulating strongly correlated systems, with an additional interest in integrating AI into scientific research workflows.

    <p class="eyebrow">CHEM 487/542</p>
    <h1>Chemical Data Science</h1>
    <p>This course explores advanced topics in data science and artificial intelligence with a focus on chemistry applications. It covers fundamentals of machine learning and data science, along with ML-guided experimental design, high-throughput screening, and AI-driven chemical discovery.</p>
